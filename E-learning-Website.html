How English Learning Works

Comprehensive curriculum for 25 languages

Halabuur Institute sets you up for success by inroducing new skills at the perfect pace and giving you opportunities to practice key words and phrases in a multiple contexts.

Faster language acquisition through immersion

Halabuur Institute structured immersion method expendites language learning Students start speaking the new language on day one, as they learn intutively through real-world images and audio from native speakers.

Develop language naturally

Every lesson in the curriculum is scaffolded to improve students' proficiency and includes listening, speaking, pronunciation, reading, grammar, vocabulary, writing, and review skills. Student start by learning a single word, then build up phrases and finally full sentences and conversations.

Build language stickiness through meaning

Our immersive approach mimics how you first learn a language, so there are English instruction or translation. Instead students learn through context and reasoning, assigning meaning to wht they see and hear. This allows students to build a deeper and more lasting understanding of the language.

Confidence Building through real conversation

Leaners spend the majority of their time in the program speaking aloud, and they receive immediate positive feedback when theire pronunciation align to that of native speakers. practicing in this safe invironment helps students feel more comfident and comfortable speaking in the real world.
